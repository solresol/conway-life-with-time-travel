Rules and Information

  • A cell can be alive if either 5 or 6 of its neighbours in the previous year and following year combined are alive.
  • Year 2 is automatically calculated. Year 3 changes colour -- blue means too few neighbours for the cell to be alive; red means too many neighbours to be alive; green means the cell has just the right neighbours and shouldn't be dead.
